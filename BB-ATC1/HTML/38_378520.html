Subject: Gilles Muller,Charles Consel,Renaud Marlet,Luciano Porto Barreto,Fabrice Merillon,Laurent Reveillere Towards Robust OSes for Appliances: A New Approach Based on Domain-Specific Languages

Description: this paper, existing OS approaches are assessed with respect to the requirements raised by appliances. The limitations of these approaches are analyzed and used as a basis to propose a new approach to designing and structuring OSes for appliances. This approach is based on Domain-Specific Languages (DSLs), and offers rapid development of robust OSes. We illustrate and assess our approach by concrete examples.
